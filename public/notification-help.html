Enable Notifications 🔔

Follow the steps below for your device to receive message notifications when the app is closed.

🤖 Android (Chrome)

1

Open PulseChat in Chrome browser

2

Tap the Install banner or Chrome menu → Add to Home Screen

3

Open the installed app → tap Allow when asked for notification permission

4

If blocked: Settings → Apps → Chrome → Notifications → Allow

🍎 iPhone / iPad (iOS 16.4+)

1

Open PulseChat in Safari (not Chrome)

2

Tap the Share button ⬆ at the bottom

3

Scroll and tap Add to Home Screen

4

Open the app from your home screen

5

Tap Allow on the notification permission popup

⚠️ iOS push notifications only work when the app is installed via Safari's "Add to Home Screen"
